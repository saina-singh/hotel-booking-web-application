Privacy Policy

This policy explains how World Hotels collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Our Commitment

At World Hotels, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how your data is collected and used.

Information We Collect
  • Name, email address, and profile details
  • Booking information (dates, hotels, rooms)
  • Payment method (no card details stored)
  • Basic site usage for performance improvements
How We Use Your Data
  • To manage hotel bookings and reservations
  • To provide customer support and assistance
  • To maintain account security
  • To improve website functionality and experience
Data Protection & Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

  • Encrypted data storage
  • Secure authentication sessions
  • Restricted internal access
  • Regular system monitoring
What We Do NOT Do
  • We do not sell your personal data
  • We do not share data with advertisers
  • We do not send spam or marketing emails
  • We do not store card or bank details
Your Trust Is Important to Us

World Hotels complies with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR. You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data.
